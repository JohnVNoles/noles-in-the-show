About Noles in the Show

Noles in the Show is a fan-run tracker dedicated to following Florida State University baseball alumni throughout their professional careers — from rookie ball all the way to the Major Leagues.

Florida State has one of the most storied baseball programs in the country. Playing out of Dick Howser Stadium in Tallahassee, the Seminoles have made 24 College World Series appearances and captured more than 20 ACC Championships. The program has consistently produced professional talent, sending over 350 players to the draft since the modern era began.

This site pulls live stats directly from the MLB Stats API and refreshes regularly throughout the season. Stats reflect current-season performance across all levels of affiliated and independent professional baseball.

Noles in the Show is a fan site and is not affiliated with Florida State University or Major League Baseball. Data sourced from the MLB Stats API.
