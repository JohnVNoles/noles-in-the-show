Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Noles in the Show ("we," "our," or "the site") is a fan-operated website that tracks Florida State University baseball alumni in professional baseball. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you visit nolesintheshow.com and how it is used.

Information We Collect

We do not require you to create an account or submit any personal information to use this site. However, the following data may be collected automatically:

  • Log data — Your browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time of visit, collected by our hosting provider (GitHub Pages).
  • Cookies — Third-party services such as Google AdSense may place cookies on your device to serve relevant advertisements.
  • Usage data — Aggregate, anonymized data about how visitors interact with the site, collected via Google Analytics if enabled.

Google AdSense & Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and device identifiers to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings or aboutads.info.

Google's use of advertising cookies is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

How We Use Information

Any automatically collected information is used solely to:

  • Operate and improve the site
  • Understand how visitors use the site (aggregate statistics only)
  • Display relevant advertisements through Google AdSense

Third-Party Services

This site relies on the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • GitHub Pages — Hosts the website
  • Google AdSense — Serves advertisements
  • MLB Stats API — Provides publicly available player statistics

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. Aggregated, anonymized site statistics may be reviewed by the site operator to improve content.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

Your Choices

  • Cookies — You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Note that some site features may not function properly without cookies.
  • Ad personalization — Visit Google Ad Settings to manage ad preferences.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
