What is the FSU Baseball Coaches Club?

The FSU Baseball Coaches Club is the primary booster organization dedicated to supporting Florida State University baseball. Membership contributions go directly toward resources that give Nole student-athletes a competitive edge — from recruiting budgets and travel costs to facility improvements and player development programs.

Every dollar raised through the Coaches Club helps Coach Link and his staff attract top talent, retain elite coaches, and build the kind of program that produces the professional players you see on this site year after year.

Coaches Club members receive exclusive benefits including behind-the-scenes access, priority seating, and recognition throughout the program — depending on your membership level. Your support is tax-deductible through the Seminole Boosters.

Why FSU Baseball Alumni Are Worth Tracking

Florida State has produced more professional baseball players than almost any other program in the country. The alumni you see on Beyond Howser — from Cal Raleigh behind the plate in Seattle to Jamie Arnold dominating Double-A hitters — got their start wearing the Garnet & Gold.

That pipeline doesn't happen by accident. It takes investment, coaching, and a culture of winning. The Coaches Club is one of the most direct ways fans can contribute to keeping that pipeline flowing.
