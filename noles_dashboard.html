About Beyond Howser

Beyond Howser is a fan-run tracker dedicated to following Florida State University baseball alumni throughout their professional careers — from rookie ball all the way to the Major Leagues.

Florida State has one of the most storied baseball programs in the country. Playing out of Dick Howser Stadium in Tallahassee, FL, the Seminoles have produced dozens of professional players at every level of the game.

This tracker pulls live stats directly from the MLB Stats API and refreshes automatically each day during the season. Player cards show current 2026 season stats, and the roster covers all known FSU alumni active in affiliated or independent professional baseball.
